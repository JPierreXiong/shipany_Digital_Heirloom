## The Probate Problem: 6-18 Months of Uncertainty

When you pass away, your assets typically go through **probate**—a legal process that validates your will and transfers ownership to your heirs. For traditional assets like real estate or bank accounts, this process takes 6-18 months.

**For cryptocurrencies, this delay can be catastrophic:**
- **Market Volatility**: Bitcoin can lose 50% of its value in months
- **Opportunity Cost**: Assets sit idle instead of being managed
- **Legal Complexity**: Courts struggle with digital asset valuation
- **Access Delays**: Heirs can't access funds during probate

## The 2026 Estate Tax Sunset: What It Means

### Current Estate Tax Exemptions

As of 2026, the federal estate tax exemption is scheduled to **sunset** (decrease) from its current high level. This means:

- **More Estates Will Be Taxed**: Lower exemption thresholds
- **Higher Tax Rates**: Increased rates for taxable estates
- **Crypto Valuation Challenges**: Courts must determine fair market value at time of death

### The Crypto Valuation Problem

Cryptocurrencies present unique challenges for estate valuation:

- **Price Volatility**: Values can fluctuate dramatically during probate
- **Multiple Exchanges**: Different exchanges may have different prices
- **Privacy Coins**: Difficult to value and track
- **DeFi Positions**: Complex to value and transfer

## Why Probate Fails for Digital Assets

### The Legal vs. Access Gap

**Legal Ownership ≠ Access**

A court can declare your heirs the legal owners of your Bitcoin, but:
- ❌ Courts cannot provide private keys
- ❌ Wills cannot contain seed phrases (public record risk)
- ❌ Probate doesn't grant technical access
- ❌ Heirs must wait for legal process to complete

### The Time Problem

**Traditional Probate Timeline:**
1. **Filing**: 1-2 weeks
2. **Notice to Creditors**: 3-4 months
3. **Asset Inventory**: 2-3 months
4. **Valuation**: 1-2 months (longer for crypto)
5. **Distribution**: 2-4 months
6. **Total**: 6-18 months

**During This Time:**
- Crypto prices can crash
- DeFi positions may expire
- NFT values can plummet
- Opportunities are lost
